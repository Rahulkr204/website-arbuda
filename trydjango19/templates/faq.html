Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Arbuda Transport Agency?

Arbuda transport Agency is a logistics company and provides services for the transportation of good via trucks.

Q2. What services do we provide?

We transport various types of goods within a circle of radius 500km.

Q3. What all goods we transport?
  • Tiles
  • Grains
  • Cotton
  • Drinks

    • You can also request any other goods to transport to admin.

Q4. What maximum weight of goods can be transported in one order?
140 tonnes.
Q5. When will my order be dispatched after requesting it?
Once physical verification is done, the order will be dispatched the same or next working day.
Q6. How do i use the services of Arbuda Transport Agency?
Services of Arbuda Transport Agency can be used by placing your order on the Arbuda Transport Agency app available on Google Play Store. You can also download the app by going to Arbuda Transport Agencyâ€™s website and clicking on the download link.
Q7. How is the safety of the goods assured by the Arbuda Transport Agency?
To assure our users that their goods are safe and are taken care of we provide our users the facility to track the exact location of the truck carrying their goods. Also there will always be a guard travelling with the truck driver in every journey.
Q8. What if the goods being transported get damaged during the journey?
We are very cautious about the goods but still if the goods are damaged during transportation, appropriate refund will be provided to the user.
Q9. How is the price decided for a particular trip?
The price will depend on,
  • The weight of the goods being transported,
  • Rental charges for the truck required for the trip,
  • Distance to be covered in the trip.
Q10. When is the payment made ?
The payment is made in two parts. A portion of it is to be paid before the start of the trip and the remain after the goods are delivered.
Q11. How is the payment made?
The payment is done either through cash or Cheque. If the amount to be paid is under Rs.50,000/- it is done in cash otherwise Cheques are used as the mode of payment.
Q12. Can order be placed on the website as well?
No, to place a order it is necessary to use the app.
Q13. Can a confirmed order be cancelled?
Yes, it can be cancelled if the cancel request is made before the goods are dispatched from the warehouse.
